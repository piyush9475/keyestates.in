Southern-Bypass

Southern Bypass is strategically located at the confluence of Metro, the extended EM Bypass, and the suburban rail network, making it easily accessible from all parts of the city. This area is rapidly gaining a reputation as one of the fastest-evolving stretches of land and has emerged as a key residential epicenter. As an extension of EM Bypass, Southern Bypass provides seamless connectivity to various parts of the city, enhancing its appeal.

The Southern Bypass area benefits from a full range of urban amenities, including healthcare, education, entertainment, and daily necessities. This extension of EM Bypass ensures residents have access to all modern facilities. Additionally, Southern Bypass offers a less polluted environment and the tranquility of a green retreat, distinguishing it from the hustle and bustle of central Kolkata

In terms of social infrastructure, accessibility, and quality of life, Southern Bypass is an upcoming hotspot. It has become a significant player in Kolkata’s real estate landscape, attracting numerous new residential developments. Residents enjoy a relatively pollution-free environment with all the comforts of modern living. The area is well-served by hospitals and healthcare facilities, ensuring that medical needs are easily met.

Overall, Southern Bypass is poised to be the next big thing in Kolkata’s real estate scene, offering a desirable blend of modern amenities and serene living conditions
