Signum Niva - Project Overview

Signum Niva – Premium 4BHK Residences on D.H. Road, Alipore

Signum Niva is an exclusive residential development located at 41D D.H. Road, offering ultra-spacious 4BHK homes crafted for modern urban living. Positioned along the highly strategic Diamond Harbour Road, the project ensures excellent connectivity across South and Central Kolkata. Residents can reach Taratala in just 5 minutes, Majherhat within 10–12 minutes, and Esplanade in 25–30 minutes. The proximity to Taratala Metro Station (5–7 minutes) significantly enhances daily commute convenience, making it ideal for working professionals and families alike.

Designed with a rare low-density concept, this premium development features only 42 residences across B+G+11 floors, ensuring privacy, superior ventilation, and a peaceful lifestyle. Developed by the reputed Signum Group, the project brings a strong trust factor backed by quality construction and timely delivery. Its standout USP lies in offering expansive 4BHK layouts (2,700–3,400 sq ft) combined with prime arterial road frontage—an increasingly rare offering in South Kolkata’s luxury segment.

From an investment perspective, Alipore is emerging as a high-growth micro-market driven by metro expansion, improved road infrastructure, and proximity to institutions like IIM Calcutta. Properties along D.H. Road are witnessing steady appreciation, making this project a strong choice for long-term capital gains and rental income. The strategic location ensures sustained demand, especially among premium homebuyers.

In conclusion, Signum Niva offers a perfect blend of luxury, connectivity, and long-term value in one of Kolkata’s most promising locations. Prices are expected to be in the premium range, reflecting its exclusivity and strategic positioning. Early-stage bookings may come with attractive pre-launch pricing benefits and flexible payment plans, making it an ideal time for buyers and investors to secure a future-ready home in South Kolkata.
