DTC Still Waters - Project Overview

DTC Still Waters – Luxury 3 & 4 BHK Apartments in New Town, Action Area III

Experience the perfect blend of luxury, serenity, and urban convenience at DTC Still Waters, an exclusive residential address by the trusted DTC Group. Located in Action Area III, (behind Infosys), New Town, this premium high-rise community offers a peaceful yet well-connected lifestyle in one of Kolkata’s fastest-growing neighbourhoods.

Prime Location with Seamless Connectivity

Situated in the eastern corridor of Kolkata, DTC Still Waters enjoys effortless connectivity to Sector V (IT Hub), Eco Park, City Centre II, and Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport through the Major Arterial Road (MAR) and Biswa Bangla Sarani. The upcoming New Garia–Airport Metro Line will further enhance convenience, making daily commutes faster and stress-free.

Modern Living Amidst Nature

Spread across 5.1 acres with 70% open space, DTC Still Waters offers an unmatched balance of greenery and modern design. The project features 3 and 4 BHK apartments ranging from 1,160 to 2,030 sq. ft., across 3 elegant towers (G+22 & G+23) with 452 spacious units, designed to maximize light, ventilation, and comfort.

Everything You Need, Right Around You

Live close to everything that matters — leading schools, reputed hospitals, IT parks, malls, and entertainment zones — all within minutes. DTC Still Waters ensures you enjoy a lifestyle that combines the tranquillity of nature with every modern amenity you deserve.
