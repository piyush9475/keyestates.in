Southern-Bypass

New Town, located on the northeastern edge of Kolkata, is a meticulously planned area under the New Town Kolkata Development Authority, within the North 24 Parganas district. This dynamic locale features a mix of residential developments, commercial and retail complexes, corporate parks, and institutional entities. Notably, the majority of residential projects in New Town are modern multi-story apartments, catering to diverse housing needs.

New Town is rapidly emerging as a significant employment hub in Kolkata, alongside the well-established Salt Lake Town V. Its strategic development is bolstered by a robust social infrastructure that includes esteemed educational institutions like Saltlake Shiksha Niketan, extensive retail spaces, and top-tier hospitals.

A key highlight of New Town is its planned layout, integrating residential, commercial, IT, and institutional areas. The town boasts a well-developed road network that ensures seamless connectivity, further enhanced by the upcoming Yellow Metro Line, which promises to improve transit options significantly.

In summary, New Town is a burgeoning area that combines modern living with a thriving commercial and institutional environment, making it a prime destination for both residents and businesses. The well-rounded infrastructure and strategic planning position New Town as a focal point for growth and development in Kolkata.
