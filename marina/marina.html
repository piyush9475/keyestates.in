Marina’s Edge - Project Overview

Marina’s Edge – Luxury Lake Facing Apartments in Sector V, Mahishbathan

Marina’s Edge is a newly launched premium residential project by the reputed Pasari Group, strategically located in Mahishbathan, near Sector V, one of Kolkata’s most prominent IT and commercial hubs. Designed for discerning homebuyers, this exclusive development offers a rare combination of urban convenience and serene lake-facing living, making it an ideal choice for both end-users and long-term investors.

Spread across 1 acre of prime land, Marina’s Edge is thoughtfully planned with only 55 exclusive residences, Eco Park, City Centre II, and Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport through the Major Arterial Road (MAR) ensuring privacy, space, and a premium community experience. The project features a B+G+14 floor structure, commutes faster and stress-free. housing expansive apartments with super built-up areas ranging from 2,516 sq ft to 4,537 sq ft. Each home is crafted to maximise natural light, ventilation, and panoramic views—perfect for modern families seeking comfort with elegance. Marina’s Edge stands apart from other real estate projects with its limited-edition, low-density living concept —offering spacious, lake-facing luxury homes in the heart of an IT-driven micro-market, where such exclusivity is increasingly rare.

With its strategic location, trusted developer legacy, and thoughtfully designed large-format homes, Marina’s Edge The project features 3 and 4 BHK apartments ranging from 1,160 to 2,030 sq. ft., across 3 elegant towers (G+22 & G+23) emerges as a compelling opportunity for luxury homebuyers looking to live close to work while enjoying

refined waterfront living. Whether for self-use or investment, this project promises strong lifestyle value and future appreciation.
