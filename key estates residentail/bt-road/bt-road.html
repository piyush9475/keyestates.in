Alipore

B.T. Road, a vital artery in northern Kolkata, serves as a crucial connector between Shyambazaar and Barrackpore. In recent years, its robust physical and social infrastructure has fueled a surge in demand. With well-developed amenities readily accessible, B.T. Road has emerged as a prime residential destination.

Adjacent localities such as Panihati, Agarpara, Ariadaha, Baranagar, and Belgharia further benefit from B.T. Road’s connectivity, making them sought-after areas for property development

B.T. Road hosts a plethora of educational institutions, including Ideal Cambridge Academy, Prarambh School, The Aryans School, Narula Institute of Technology, and Kamarhati Sagore Dutta Free High School. This enhances the area’s appeal to families seeking quality education for their children.

The combination of robust physical infrastructure, well-equipped social amenities, and a variety of residential options, including houses and multi-story apartments, positions B.T. Road as a lucrative investment opportunity and a desirable living destination in Kolkata.
