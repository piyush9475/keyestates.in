EM Bypass

Located on the eastern side of Kolkata, the EM Bypass is a vital 32 km long thoroughfare. This key road connects to significant areas such as Salt Lake City and New Town. Nearby localities include New Garia, Panchasayar, Kayalpara, East Balia, and Faridabad. Over the years, EM Bypass has attracted several prominent developers who have established sophisticated residential complexes, offering elegant apartments, independent houses, and multi-story residences

EM Bypass is also home to numerous esteemed educational institutions. Notable schools in and around EM Bypass include Foundation School, St Mary’s School, Rockford Re School, Welland Gouldsmith School, Briji A.T Naskar High School, Riverdale High School, and Pathmanjil Primary School. These institutions contribute to the high living standards that the flats in EM Bypass provide to residents.

The ongoing infrastructural developments along EM Bypass have significantly increased the area’s appeal, leading to a surge in demand for residential projects. Whether you’re looking for luxurious apartments or independent houses, EM Bypass offers diverse housing options that cater to refined lifestyles.
