Alipore

Nestled in the southern suburbs of Kolkata, Alipore is a sought-after residential neighborhood renowned for its excellent road connectivity and proximity to key areas. Accessible via Burdwan Road, New Road, Judges Ct Road, and Alipore Road, it is conveniently located near the Behala Industrial Estate. Alipore’s strategic location ensures seamless connections to nearby localities such as Mominpore, Kalighat, Behala, Taratala, and Tollygunge. The area features a mix of multi-story apartments and independent houses, catering to diverse housing preferences.

Alipore is home to prominent educational institutions including Loyola High School, Alipore Multipurpose Government Girls School, St. Joseph & Marya, and DAV Public School, making it an ideal choice for families. The neighborhood boasts a reliable bus service and is served by the Alipore Railway Station. Key roads like Judges Ct Road and Kalighat Road provide direct access to the Netaji Bhavan Metro Station (Blue Line), located just 2 km away.

Furthermore, Alipore offers excellent connectivity to major employment hubs such as Salt Lake via both road and metro networks. Its superior road infrastructure ensures smooth commutes, enhancing the appeal of Alipore as a prime residential destination in Kolkata.
