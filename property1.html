DTC Downtown - Project Overview

Premium 2, 3 & 4 BHK Apartments in Rajarhat Main Road

DTC Downtown is a premium residential project located on Rajarhat Main Road, offering excellent connectivity to Chinar Park, Airport, Sector V, and New Town. Spread across 10 acres, it features modern high-rise living with spacious apartments, strong infrastructure growth, and high appreciation potential.

Positioned on a 6-lane expressway, the project ensures seamless access to VIP Road, Salt Lake, and major IT hubs. Daily essentials like schools, hospitals, and malls are within easy reach, making it ideal for end-users and investors.

Developed by the trusted DTC Group, the project offers well-designed 2, 3 & 4BHK homes with natural ventilation and modern planning. With strong rental demand and resale value, it stands as a smart investment choice in Kolkata.
